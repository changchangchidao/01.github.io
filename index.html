ABOUT

Panda Cafe

Beginning in 1995, the owners established this cafe for the sole purpose of donating a portion of the profit. The Panda Menu donates all proceeds while 4% of all profit is also donated.

Returning the joy pandas give us is the best thing we can do to support them. We hope our customers will be happy knowing how much help their money gives.
